Angel Mathenjwa

First of Her Kind: The Creative Developer

Angel Mathenjwa, born on the 8th of October 2002 in Nelspruit, South Africa, and raised in Barberton, is an ambitious Junior Software Developer and Graphic Designer. She completed her primary and secondary education in Barberton and has successfully completed her tertiary studies at Eduvos, with graduation anticipated in 2025. As a Top Achiever at Eduvos in 2022, Angel has demonstrated her commitment to academic excellence and passion for innovation in software development.

Since 2020, Angel has successfully run her creative business, Unique Visionary Graphic Designs, offering branding and print solutions that leave a lasting impact. From designing logos to crafting brand identities, her work reflects creativity, attention to detail, and a deep understanding of her clients' needs. Her ability to integrate design with technology highlights her entrepreneurial spirit and versatility.

Recently, Angel joined the renowned Tshwane Gospel Choir as a musician, further showcasing her creativity and passion for music. As an aspiring software developer, Angel dreams of working with leading global technology companies, where she can contribute innovative solutions and continue to grow her expertise. She envisions mentoring the next generation of developers and designers and using her skills to make a meaningful impact in the tech world.
